Hangman





Welcome to Hangman, a word-guessing game where you try to figure out a hidden word by guessing one letter at a time! Each time you guess a wrong letter, a part of the hangman figure is drawn, and the goal is to guess the word before the figure is fully drawn. You continue guessing letters, with each correct guess filling in part of the word, while incorrect guesses add more parts to the hangman.

The challenging part lies in balancing your guesses, as every wrong choice brings you closer to losing the game. The word is usually represented by blank spaces, and you must deduce the letters through logic or pattern recognition. Successfully guessing all the letters before the hangman is drawn means you win, but if the figure is completed, you lose.
